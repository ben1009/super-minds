📚 Grammar Focus · Page 108 语法重点 🎯

1. "It's time for..." 句式

结构: It's time for + 名词
意思: 该做某事了 / 到做某事的时间了

例子:

  • It's time for a holiday. (该放假了)
  • It's time for dinner. (该吃晚饭了)
  • It's time for school. (该上学了)
注意: 不要说 "It's time for back"，正确是 "It's time to go back" (该回去了)。
因为 for 后面接名词，to 后面接动词。
2. "Can I / Can we..." 句式 (请求允许)

结构: Can + 主语 + 动词原形

各人称完整列表:

weCan we go camping?我们可以去露营吗？

sheCan she go swimming?她可以去游泳吗？

theyCan they sleep in the tree house?他们能睡在树屋里吗？
歌词中的例子: Can I build a tree house...? · Can I go camping...? · Can I go swimming...? · Can I take riding lessons...?
3. "I am going to..." 句式 (打算做某事)

结构: 主语 + be going to + 动词原形 (be: am/is/are)
意思: 打算 / 将要去做某事

各人称变化: I am going to / We/You/They are going to / He/She/It is going to
  • I am going to sleep. (我打算睡觉)
  • ❌ I going to sleep (X) ---- 不要忘记 be 动词！
  • You are going to play. (你将要玩)
  • He/She/It is going to join me. (他/她/它将要加入)
  • We are going to build a tree house. (我们打算建树屋)
  • They are going to go camping. (他们打算去露营)

📝 Page 109 · 语法总结 📚

注意: 特殊疑问句的结构 = 疑问词 + 一般疑问句语序 (或 + 系动词/助动词 + 主语 + ...)

一、一般现在时

1. 系动词 (be): am / is / are

问句结构: 疑问词 + be + 主语...?
  • Q2: How old is your brother?
  • Q5: What is her name? (缩写 What's)
  • Q6: Where are my pencils?
  • Q8: Where are my pens?
  • Q11: When is her birthday?
  • Q12: Whose jumper is pink?

2. 实义动词 (do / does 帮助构成疑问句)

结构: Do/Does + 主语 + 动词原形...?
第三人称单数用 Does，动词恢复原形 (如 eat, like)。
  • Q1: Does your sister eat tomatoes? (实义动词 eat)
  • Q7: What does your sister like to eat? (实义动词 like)
回答: 肯定: Yes, she does. / 否定: No, she doesn't.

二、can 句型 (情态动词)

结构: Can + 主语 + 动词原形...?
  • 问句: Q3. Can you swim?
  • 回答: Yes, I can. / No, I can't.
其他情态动词类似: would like (Q9. Would you like an orange? 回答: Yes, please.)

三、have got 句型 (表示"有")

结构: Have/Has + 主语 + got ...?
  • 问句: Q4. Have spiders got six legs?
注意: 答句不直接用 have got，而用 have/has。本题中答句 "No, they've got a cat." (= No, they have got a cat.) 实际完整否定回答应为: No, they haven't. 但题目幽默地用了"不，他们有猫"。

对比: have got = have (有)，但疑问和否定更常用 have got 形式。

四、there be 句型

结构: Is/Are there + 名词 + 地点/范围?
  • 问句: Q10. Is there any milk in the house?
  • 回答: Yes, there is. / No, there isn't.

Dialogue Fill-in-the-Blanks (对话填空) - Tom & Anna

场景：Tom 和 Anna 讨论假期计划

Tom:Hi, Anna!

Anna:Hello, Tom. _____ (1) are you?

Tom:I'm fine, thanks. Tomorrow is a holiday.

Anna:Great! _____ (2) are you going to do?

Tom:I'm going to my grandma's house.

Anna:_____ (3) is your grandma's house?

Tom:It's near the beach.

Anna:Cool! _____ (4) are you going with?

Tom:My cousin, Lily.

Anna:_____ (5) old is Lily?

Tom:She's ten.

Anna:_____ (6) don't you go swimming?

Tom:Good idea! But first, _____ (7) jumper is that on the chair? Is it yours?

Anna:No, it's Lily's. I think it's her birthday next week.

Tom:_____ (8) is her birthday?

Anna:In July.

Tom:_____ (9) there any drinks in the fridge?

Anna:Yes, there _____ (10) some juice.

Tom:_____ (11) your sister like apples?

Anna:No, she _____ (12). She likes oranges.

Tom:_____ (13) you swim?

Anna:Yes, I _____ (14).

Tom:_____ (15) spiders got eight legs?

Anna:No, they _____ (16) eight legs? Actually they have eight legs - wait, yes!

Tom:Haha, you're right.

Anna:_____ (17) you like an ice cream?

Tom:Yes, _____ (18). Let's go!

题号答案考查内容
1How特殊疑问词 (问候 How are you?)
2What特殊疑问词 (what ... do)
3Where特殊疑问词 (地点)
4Who特殊疑问词 (人物)
5How特殊疑问词 (how old 年龄)
6Why特殊疑问词 (原因)
7Whose特殊疑问词 (所属)
8When特殊疑问词 (时间)
9Isthere be 句型 (Is there...)
10isthere be 肯定回答 (there is)
11Does一般现在时 (实义动词 like, 第三人称单数)
12doesn't一般现在时否定回答
13Cancan 句型 (能力)
14cancan 肯定回答
15Havehave got 句型 (Have spiders got...)
16haven't got注意：这里故意设计幽默，但标准答案用 "haven't got" 或者直接 "No, they have."
17Wouldwould like 句型 (Would you like...)
18please礼貌回答 (Yes, please.)

Dialogue: Planning the Holiday (对话: Amy & Ben)

场景：Amy 和 Ben 计划假期

Amy:Hi, Ben! The holiday is coming.

Ben:Yes! _____ (1) are you going to do?

Amy:I'm going to my grandma's house.

Ben:_____ (2) does your grandma live?

Amy:She lives near a hill. She has a big apple tree.

Ben:Wow! _____ (3) are you going to build in that tree?

Amy:A tree house! I'm going to sleep and play there.

Ben:Cool! _____ (4) are you going to build it with?

Amy:My cousins, Michael, James and Lynn.

Ben:_____ (5) old are they?

Amy:Michael is 11, James is 10, and Lynn is 9.

Ben:_____ (6) don't you go camping, too?

Amy:Good idea! We can go camping on a sandy beach.

Ben:_____ (7) beach is it? Is it Sandy Bay?

Amy:Yes. And I'm going swimming there.

Ben:Can I join you?

Amy:Of course! _____ (8) you swim?

Ben:Yes, I _____ (9).

Amy:Great! _____ (10) there any sharks in the sea?

Ben:No, there _____ (11) any sharks near Sandy Bay.

Amy:Phew! Look, _____ (12) jumper is that on the chair?

Ben:It's Lynn's. She's taking riding lessons.

Amy:Riding lessons? _____ (13) does she take riding lessons?

Ben:On Saturday mornings.

Amy:I want to take riding lessons too!

Ben:You can join them. _____ (14) your sister like horses?

Amy:Yes, she _____ (15). She loves horses.

Ben:Perfect! _____ (16) you like an ice cream now?

Amy:Yes, _____ (17). Let's go!
