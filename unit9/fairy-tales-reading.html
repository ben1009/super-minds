📖 Reading A · Folk Tales Around the World 🌍

Folk tales are old stories from different countries. They often have heroes, magic, and talking animals.

民间故事是来自不同国家的古老故事。它们通常有英雄、魔法和会说话的动物。

Many folk tales teach a lesson or explain how something began.

许多民间故事传授道理，或者解释某事是如何开始的。

A famous writer of fairy tales is Hans Christian Andersen from Denmark. He wrote The Ugly Duckling, The Little Match Girl, and The Emperor's New Clothes.

一位著名的童话作家是来自丹麦的汉斯·克里斯蒂安·安徒生。他写了《丑小鸭》《卖火柴的小女孩》和《皇帝的新装》。

These stories are loved by children and adults around the world.

这些故事深受全世界儿童和成人的喜爱。

In China, people tell the story of Chang'e and the Moon. In Arabia, there is One Thousand and One Nights with stories like Aladdin (which also became a Disney film).

在中国，人们讲述嫦娥奔月的故事。在阿拉伯，有《一千零一夜》，里面有像阿拉丁这样的故事（后来也被改编成了迪士尼电影）。

Folk tales help us understand different cultures. They also make us dream and feel hopeful.

民间故事帮助我们了解不同的文化。它们也让我们拥有梦想并感到充满希望。

📝 Reading Comprehension · 阅读理解

1. What are folk tales?

A. News reports about different countries
B. Old stories with heroes, magic, or talking animals
C. Science textbooks for children
D. Cooking recipes from around the world
答案: B
根据文章第一句："Folk tales are old stories from different countries. They often have heroes, magic, and talking animals."

2. Which story was written by Hans Christian Andersen?

A. Aladdin
B. Chang'e and the Moon
C. The Ugly Duckling
D. One Thousand and One Nights
答案: C
文中提到："He wrote The Ugly Duckling, The Little Match Girl, and The Emperor's New Clothes."

3. Why do people like folk tales according to the passage?

A. Because they are very long and difficult
B. Because they help us understand different cultures and make us dream
C. Because they are only for adults
D. Because they all come from Disney films
答案: B
文章最后一句："Folk tales help us understand different cultures. They also make us dream and feel hopeful."

4. Where does the story of Chang'e and the Moon come from?

A. Denmark
B. Arabia
C. China
D. Germany
答案: C
文中明确说："In China, people tell the story of Chang'e and the Moon."

📖 Reading B · Reading on Holiday 🏖️

Many people like to read on holiday. They choose different kinds of books and magazines.

许多人喜欢在度假时阅读。他们选择不同种类的书籍和杂志。

Some people read story books or novels. Others read comics or magazines.

有些人读故事书或小说。其他人读漫画或杂志。

On holiday, you have more free time. You can sit on a beach or in a park and read.

在假期，你有更多的空闲时间。你可以坐在海滩上或公园里阅读。

Popular holiday reads are detective stories, love stories, and science fiction. These stories help you relax and escape from daily life.

受欢迎的假期读物有侦探故事、爱情故事和科幻小说。这些故事帮助你放松，逃离日常生活。

Children often read fairy tales or adventure books. Adults may read crime novels or travel guides.

孩子们经常读童话故事或冒险书籍。成年人可能读犯罪小说或旅游指南。

No matter what you read, reading on holiday makes the trip more enjoyable.

无论你读什么，在假期阅读都会让旅行更加愉快。

So next time you pack your suitcase, don't forget to bring a book!

所以下次你收拾行李箱时，别忘了带一本书！

📝 Reading Comprehension · 阅读理解

1. What do people often do on holiday according to the passage?

A. Go to work
B. Read books or magazines
C. Write detective stories
D. Watch films only
答案: B
文章第一句："Many people like to read on holiday. They choose different kinds of books and magazines."

2. Why is reading on holiday enjoyable?

A. Because books are cheap
B. Because you can escape daily life and relax
C. Because you have to study
D. Because comics are difficult
答案: B
文中提到："These stories help you relax and escape from daily life."

3. What do children like to read on holiday?

A. Crime novels
B. Travel guides
C. Fairy tales or adventure books
D. Science reports
答案: C
文中明确说："Children often read fairy tales or adventure books."

4. What does the writer suggest at the end?

A. Buy many magazines at the beach
B. Leave your books at home
C. Bring a book when you pack your suitcase
D. Only read detective stories
答案: C
文章最后一句："So next time you pack your suitcase, don't forget to bring a book!"
