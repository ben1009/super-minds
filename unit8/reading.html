📖 Reading Text · 课文原文

点击段落查看翻译 · Click text to see translation

My classmates and I enjoy doing different activities. Each of us has a favourite thing to do.

🌐 点击显示翻译
我和我的同学们喜欢做不同的活动。我们每个人都有自己喜欢做的事情。

Amy likes painting in her free time. She often paints beautiful flowers. Creating something with colours makes her feel peaceful.

🌐 点击显示翻译
艾米喜欢在她的空闲时间画画。她经常画美丽的花朵。用颜色创造东西让她感到平静。

Ben enjoys flying a kite on windy days. He says running across the grass and watching the kite go up high is really exciting.

🌐 点击显示翻译
本喜欢在刮风的日子放风筝。他说在草地上奔跑，看着风筝高高飞起真的很令人兴奋。

Cindy loves riding her bike around the park. She rides almost every afternoon. Cycling helps her stay healthy and strong.

🌐 点击显示翻译
辛迪喜欢在公园周围骑自行车。她几乎每天下午都骑车。骑自行车帮助她保持健康和强壮。

David is interested in making model planes. Building them takes time, but he feels proud when he finishes one.

🌐 点击显示翻译
大卫对制作飞机模型感兴趣。制作它们需要时间，但当他完成一个时，他感到自豪。

What about me? I like listening to music while reading stories. Doing two things together is fun for me.

🌐 点击显示翻译
那我呢？我喜欢一边听音乐一边读故事。同时做两件事对我来说很有趣。

📚 Grammar Focus · 语法重点

🎯 Like / Enjoy / Love + V-ing

喜欢/享受/热爱做某事

  • ● Amy likes painting
  • ● Ben enjoys flying a kite
  • ● Cindy loves riding her bike
  • ● I like listening to music

🎯 Be interested in + V-ing

对...感兴趣

David is interested in making model planes.

大卫对制作飞机模型感兴趣。

💡 Tip: "in" 是介词，后面动词要加 -ing

🎯 Help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事

Cycling helps her stay healthy.

骑自行车帮助她保持健康。
