My classmates and I enjoy doing different activities. Each of us has a favourite thing to do.

我和我的同学们喜欢做不同的活动。我们每个人都有自己喜欢做的事情。

📚 Grammar Focus · 语法重点

🎯 Like / Enjoy / Love + V-ing

喜欢/享受/热爱做某事

  • ● Amy likes painting
  • ● Ben enjoys flying a kite
  • ● Cindy loves riding her bike
  • ● I like listening to music

🎯 Be interested in + V-ing

对...感兴趣

David is interested in making model planes.

大卫对制作飞机模型感兴趣。

💡 Tip: "in" 是介词，后面动词要加 -ing

🎯 Help sb. (to) do sth. 帮助某人做某事

Cycling helps her stay healthy.

骑自行车帮助她保持健康。
